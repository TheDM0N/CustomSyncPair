Tips and Advice

▼
  • Click on areas with words in order to edit them
  • Change colors and levels with the selectors below the image
  • Click on Gmax move to add it to the photo the editor selector will appear below the move selectors
  • Click on upload trainer to upload a trainer image(note: use a png with no extra space for best results)
  • Pokemon Image can be used by searching for the pokemons ID number (which is their numerical number on bulbapedia)
  • You can enter the number 0 to have a blank area to add your own image afterwards(Mega evolutions and other forms have to do this method for now)
  • Bulbapedia should have high quality pngs of all characters and pokemon if searched
  • When finished take a screenshot of the area, if using on desktop and using a Gmax move, enter full screen mode to fit it all
