Tips and Advice

▼
  • Click on areas with words in order to edit them
  • Change colors and levels with the selectors below the image
  • Click on upload trainer to upload a trainer image(note use a png with no extra space is preferable)
  • When finished take a screenshot of the area
  • For now the pokemon image must be added on your own device after screenshoting
  • Bulbapedia should have high quality pngs of all characters and pokemon if searched
